For each match in the queue:
	Determine which available times the two teams have in common
	For common available times (if any):
		Check through all teams' availability to determine how many other teams have the same available time slot
		**Schedule match for time with lowest common preferences**

	If no common available times:
		Compare first team's availability with other teams (prioritizes lower availability)
		**Schedule match for time with lowest common preferences**
